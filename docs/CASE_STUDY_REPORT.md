## 1) Roshan Enterprises E-Commerce

Observed live behavior:
- Strong hero headline and clear value proposition around premium everyday essentials.
- Category browsing is immediate and product cards show price, stock, and cart actions.
- Trust signals are visible through fast delivery, secure checkout, and product quality messaging.

Strengths:
- Good conversion flow from hero to collection to cart.
- Product density is high enough for a retail demo without feeling empty.
- The site communicates business purpose clearly.

Recommended case-study angle:
- Focus on how you supported both retail and wholesale journeys.
- Call out checkout flow, product browsing, and trust-building UI.

## 2) Lumi Glow

Observed live behavior:
- The home page uses motion, scroll sequencing, and premium storytelling.
- The page emphasizes interaction depth, product carousels, and CTA hierarchy.
- The design feels more editorial and conversion-oriented than a normal catalog page.

Strengths:
- Strong visual differentiation.
- Clear progression from hook to engage to convert.
- Works well as a design-forward case study.

Recommended case-study angle:
- Present it as a conversion-first storefront with premium motion and guided browsing.
- Highlight scroll momentum, CTA design, and product storytelling.

## 3) Search Algorithm Simulator

Observed live behavior:
- The live page starts with a locked terminal-style login screen.
- The brand feel is more game-like and mission-driven than a standard learning app.
- The UI suggests gated access and a guided learning experience.

Strengths:
- Strong thematic direction.
- Distinct from typical algorithm visualizers.
- Useful for showing problem-solving and educational product thinking.

Recommended case-study angle:
- Frame it as an interactive learning tool for DSA concepts.
- Emphasize simulations, guided explanation, and a memorable interface.

## 4) CampusShare / Study Archive

Observed live behavior:
- The deployed page currently presents a login-first course management and resource sharing experience.
- It is functional and simple, but the live screen is mostly entry-gated.

Strengths:
- Clear academic utility.
- Suitable for demonstrating Firebase-backed auth and resource management.

Recommended case-study angle:
- Explain it as a student resource hub with auth and centralized content access.
- If available, include screenshots of the post-login flow for the case study.

## 5) Angular Bakery App

Observed live behavior:
- The homepage has a clean bakery-style hero with a simple product-driven structure.
- It communicates freshness, handcrafted quality, and same-day delivery.
- Additional links show the project extends beyond the landing page into products and ordering.

Strengths:
- Easy to understand.
- Clear ecommerce intent.
- Strong for showing multi-page product flow.

Recommended case-study angle:
- Present it as a bakery commerce app with product discovery and ordering.
- Highlight the branded hero, product navigation, and delivery messaging.

## Overall Portfolio Recommendation

- You build real deployed products.
- You care about both design and usable flows.
- You can show live demos, not just screenshots.
